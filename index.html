Mi Abuela Tribute Page.

"Mi Abuela"

"1928 - 2016"

A comer pastel, a comer lechon, arroz con gandules, y a beber ron.

Some of her favorite foods and drinks to make for the holidays:

  1. "Arroz con Dulce" or Rice Pudding
  2. "Pasteles" or Carribean Style Meat Pie
  3. "Pernil" or Pork Shoulder
  4. "Mabi" or Puerto Rican Champagne
  5. "Coquito" or Puerto Rican Eggnog
